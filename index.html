Popular Destination

Sanur Beach

Sanur Beach may not rank up there with Bali’s top beaches, but it certainly shines (no pun intended) as one of the best sunrise spots on the island.

Nusa Penida

Nusa Penida is an island South-East of the Indonesian island Bali. Nusa Penida is only a thirty minute boat ride from Bali. The island’s landscape is beautiful, the diving spots are world class and its people are very welcoming. Until recently, the island was still relatively unknown by by tourists.

Buyan Lake

Buyan Lake is one of the four lakes in Bali that offers spectacular natural view to refresh our soul and mind. It is a nice place to spend a day or two camping trip while you are on vacation on the island of paradise - Bali.
